MINI PROJECTS


Title1:NON-INVASIVE BLOOD GLUCOSE MONITORING TECHNOLOGY

 

Sweat using softbioelectronics on human skin provideds a new route for non invasive glucose monitoring without painful collection of body. Diabetes Mellitus is a major health concern in the society today both locally and worldwide. Diabetes has been linked with other health problems, including heartdisease and stroke, kidney disease, nerve damage and vision loss due to fluctuating blood sugar levels.

Title2:IOT BASED SMART ENERY METER

IOT based smart energy meter to track the energy consumption automatically of the residential load. This meter is capable of sending the consumptions to the consumer as well as an electricity supplier. The readings are taken automatically byusing the HLW8012 sensor. Then a predefined set of programs calculates the total bill of energy consumed over the selected interval using the ESP32 microcontroller



MAJOR PROJECT


Title: Energy efficient AES Forward Mix column using Reversible Logic

The objective of this project is to explore the use of reversible logic to implementing the AES forward mix column operation in order to reduce power consumption and improve energy efficiency. Reversible logic is one of the most important issues at the moment and has a wide range of applications, such as low power CMOS, quantum computing, nanotechnology, cryptography, optical computing, DNA computing, and -digital signal processing (DSP), quantum Dot automata for mobile, communication, computer graphics. The main aim of this design for logical thinking to reduce quantum costs, circuit depths and the amount of waste disposal. Reversible circuits form the basic building block of quantum computers as all quantum functions are reversed.
